Hello! I'm Mindy, a web developer based in Brooklyn, NY. I write a lot of JavaScript, HTML, CSS and, depending on the client, Python and PHP. I started making websites in high school when I got my first 30-day-AOL-trial CD in the mail. The 14-year-old me was so enamored by the “magic” in creating websites and since then my love for building things for the web has only grown (yes, I realize how cheesy this sounds, but it's true!).

I work part-time as a developer and designer for Scripto, a TV-production software company. I spend the rest of my time freelancing, teaching kids how to code (via a wonderful program called ScriptEd), and working as a barista (on purpose). My jobs as a developer, teacher, and barista may seem scattered, but they all nurture two of my most important values– making meaningful things and helping people. Previously I've worked for Yelp, CNET, and Reuters.

Please feel free to say hello at mtchieu@gmail.com.
